The Avni April releases (v16.10.0 through v16.12.0) bring action-driven dashboards, richer mobile reports, and a quick-open path into the app from WhatsApp. Here are the highlights.

## Custom cards with configurable actions

Custom cards can now trigger actions directly from a dashboard, so users can act on a card without first navigating into a subject. The supported actions are **Do Visit** and **View Subject Profile**.

## More power to report cards in mobile

Reports in the mobile app can now show custom visualisations like tables, pie charts, or bar graphs. Tapping the card opens a dedicated page tailored to the report, with support for local-language content so partner organisations can shape the layout to their needs.

## Open the Avni app via a WhatsApp link

Tapping `https://app.avniproject.org/open/` from WhatsApp now opens the Avni app directly — landing on the login screen if the user is signed out, or the home page otherwise. If the app isn't installed, the link falls through to the Play Store.
